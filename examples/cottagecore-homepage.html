about this corner

A small cottage, a large garden, and a very slow life

I'm Mae — a forager, bread-baker, and chronic embroiderer living in a weathered stone cottage on the edge of a wood. This is where I document the seasons: what's growing, what's in the pot, and what I'm stitching by lamplight in the evenings.

Everything here is made by hand, found in hedgerows, or preserved in glass jars with handwritten labels. No shortcuts. No synthetics.
