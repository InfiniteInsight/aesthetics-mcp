Loadout Protocol

SUPPLY CHECKLIST

The minimum viable loadout before leaving any safe zone. No exceptions.

🎒 Essential Carry

  • Water — minimum 3L per person
  • High-calorie rations — 3 days
  • First-aid kit with antibiotics
  • Radio — charged, correct freq
  • Backup batteries or solar charger
  • Terrain map — paper, not digital
  • Emergency flare or signal mirror

⚔️ Defense & Tools

  • Primary weapon — inspected, loaded
  • Blade — secondary melee
  • Rope — 15m minimum
  • Multi-tool or folding saw
  • Fire starter — two methods
  • Headlamp — red-light mode available
  • Thermal layer — even in summer
